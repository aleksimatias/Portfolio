DIGIREACTOR

The app was a school project for DigiReactor, which is in co-operation
with TUAS, University of Turku and Turku Business Region.

The application was done by a student group using React Native.
I created the initial UI mockups, and during the development phase
I was tasked with creating different page and menu views.

FRIBAKELI

Fribakeli was a school project done in a group of 3 students.
The application combined local weather data with coordinates of
disc golf tracks to let users find viable tracks to play on.

The application was created using React.
I worked on the weather data, map styling and page elements.

Fribakeli is currently a WIP project, and it may be finished this year.

KILTA

Kilta is a single-player auto battler strategy RPG by MyTrueSound.

I worked on video content, such as trailers and gameplay previews.
I worked mainly with Premiere Pro, After Effects and Vegas Pro.

The game is currently available in Early Access on Steam
and it will be launched worldwide during Q1 of 2022.

VIRPA - FIRE EXPERT

VirPa is a digital learning environment in the form of a mobile game created in TUAS.
It teaches kids aged 8-13 the required knowledge, skills and attitudes towards fire safety.
The project was funded by the Finnish Ministry of Interior through the Fire Protection Fund.

I worked on video content, such as trailers and gameplay previews with Premiere Pro,
After Effects and Audition. My work can be seen on the project's website.

The game is free to download from App Store and Google Play.
